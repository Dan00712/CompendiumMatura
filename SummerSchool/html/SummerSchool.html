\(m = 2t\)
\(m_1 = \frac{2}{3}t\)

\(E_{kin} = \frac{1}{2} m v^2\)
\(E_{kin; ges} = const = \frac{1}{2} m_1 v_1^2 + \frac{1}{2} m_2 v_2^2 + E_{BOOM} = \frac{1}{2} m_1 v_1'^2 + \frac{1}{2} m_2 v_2'^2\)

\(E_{BOOM} = 100kJ = \frac{1}{2} m_1 v_1'^2 + \frac{1}{2} m_2 v_2'^2\)
